Political Career Tracker



Welcome to the Political Career Tracker (PCT)!

PCT is an online tool developed by the Trivedi Centre of Political Data to track the career trajectory of India's elected representatives and candidates to state and national elections since 1962. The data is based on the coding of a unique identifying number for every candidate, thanks to TCPD's tool Surf. This data interface helps you visualize all candidates contesting an election and enable you to filter these candidates by various categories: first-time contestants, re-running incumbents, turncoats, among others. The data interface also provides information on the number of terms served by MPs and MLAs, and the number of time candidates have contested.

This data and the visualization are free to use and can be downloaded. We encourage students, researchers, journalists, policy makers and whoever else interested in Indian politicians' careers to engage with our data. Should you have any query and/or notice an error, please send us a message or write us at tcpd-contact@ashoka.edu.in. We value your feedback and recommend that you read the documentation before using the tool.
